SAHYOG

Sahyog

Sahyog is aimed at providing all possible assistance to the underprivileged community by conducting various distribution drives across the city. It is a year round campaign when our team comes on ground to cover the basic necessities and provide maximum support to the public.


  • Sahyog 21’: In the midst of lockdown 2021: Our team distributed 110+ covid protection kits to 50+ families in and around Gorakhpur. These families had no permanent shelter and resources to protect themselves. Along with this our team worked round the clock to provide oxygen cylinder support to critical cases in the hospitals and provide oxygen bed support to the needy. We maintained and circulated critical contact data during the covid crises. The organization also managed to distribute 15 days of ration to 10 families who were facing the consequences of income and livelihood loss due to lockdown.

  • Sahyog 22’ - Refreshment Distribution (May’22-Jun’22): SAHYOG 22 was about extending our gratitude to those who provide their services for the society despite various difficulties, such as harsh weather weather conditions- excess heat, rain, etc. The team went down on roads and extended sincere thanks to all the traffic policemen and frontline workers. Their hard work despite all odds was acknowledged and they were provided with Amul Buttermilk amidst the hot summer to help them hydrate and refresh themselves.We covered 24 areas around the city and distributed 250 buttermilk to around 100 policemen and 20 frontline workers.
