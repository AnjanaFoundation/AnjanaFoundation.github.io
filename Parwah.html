PARVAAH

Parvaah

Parvaah is dedicated to assisting the less fortunate through charitable efforts, extending a helping hand to those in need, and fostering compassion and support for underprivileged individuals and communities.


  • Let’s spread the warmth: Our team distributed 110+ covid protection kits to 50+ families in and around Gorakhpur. These families had no permanent shelter and resources to protect themselves. Along with this our team worked round the clock to provide oxygen cylinder support to critical cases in the hospitals and provide oxygen bed support to the needy. We maintained and circulated critical contact data during the covid crises. The organization also managed to distribute 15 days of ration to 10 families who were facing the consequences of income and livelihood loss due to lockdown.

  • Period Power (Aug’22): A menstruation advocacy and sanitary napkin distribution drive where the team educated the ladies and the young girls on menstrual hygiene . Our team distributed around 200 packets of sanitary napkins to teenagers and ladies at various localities and schools.
  • Raktdaan (June '23): Anjana Foundation orchestrated a productive collaborative blood donation drive alongside Shri Guru Goraksnath Blood Bank. In its inaugural phase, the initiative garnered a commendable collection of 14 vital blood units, contributing significantly to life-saving efforts.
