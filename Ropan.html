ROPAN

Ropan

'Ropan' endeavors to cultivate environmental sustainability through tree planting, green space ceation, and raising awareness about the vital role we play in preserving our ecosystem for a healthier and greener future.


  • Ropan (Jul’21)- Nurturing life amidst heatwaves. A resolute team braved scorching temperatures to breathe life into our city's landscape. Our initiative led to the planting of 178 saplings in the 'Nausad' region of Gorakhpur, each bearing the name of a dedicated contributor. Moreover, we motivated individuals throughout the month to embrace home plantations via our social media channels.
  • Ropan (Jul’22) - In the second year of our campaign, Ropan extended its reach to the younger generation by organizing the plantation drive within schools. In this edition, our team successfully contributed to planting 212 saplings, with valuable assistance from school children. This collaborative effort not only bolstered our cause but also instilled the values of environmental care and protection in the children.
